Our Legacy

Engineering-led from day one.

Founded in 1968, Precision Electronics Instruments & Components began with a clear mandate: engineer medical sterilization systems that hospitals and institutions could rely on for years of daily operation.

That manufacturing foundation expanded from BIS-certified autoclave production into technical integration for global and Indian medical equipment brands. The distribution business is built on equipment understanding, not catalogue selling.

Today, PEIC gives procurement and biomedical teams clearer ownership across specification, installation, commissioning, documentation, and long-term post-sale support.
