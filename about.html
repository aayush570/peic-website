Our Legacy

Over Five Decades of Precision Engineering

Founded in 1968, Precision Electronics Instruments & Components began with a singular mission: to engineer medical sterilization systems that hospitals could depend on for decades. What started as a focused manufacturing operation has grown into a comprehensive technical solutions provider serving both massive government healthcare networks and elite private hospital chains across India.

Our evolution reflects the changing landscape of Indian healthcare — from the early days of BIS-certified autoclave manufacturing to today's role as authorized integration partner for global medical technology leaders. Through every transition, our commitment to precision, safety, and reliability has remained unchanged.

Today, PEIC stands at the intersection of indigenous manufacturing excellence and world-class distribution — a unique position that allows us to offer procurement heads and biomedical engineers a single, accountable partner for their entire equipment lifecycle.
